Privacy Policy

Helix — AR Pet Companion  |  Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Helix is an AR pet companion app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Helix, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app experience.

  • Account Information — When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Pet & Game Data — Your pet's name, type, stats, tasks, and progress are stored so your experience persists across sessions.
  • Usage Data — Basic app activity such as tasks completed and care actions taken.
  • Google Sign-In — If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google. We do not receive your Google password.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To save and sync your pet's data across devices
  • To generate personalized AI tasks for your pet
  • To improve the app experience over time

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides encrypted cloud storage and authentication. Firebase complies with industry-standard security practices. Data is stored on Firebase servers and is protected by Google's security infrastructure.

Camera & AR

Helix uses your device camera to enable augmented reality features. Camera data is processed on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Helix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time
  • You may update your account information within the app

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at helixfbla@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at helixfbla@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours.
