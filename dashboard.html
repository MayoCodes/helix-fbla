Features

Welcome to HELIX

Your all-in-one virtual pet care and tracking platform.

HELIX is a dashboard that connects to your companion app to give you a real-time view of your virtual pet's health, happiness, and your spending. Everything you do in the app — feeding, playing, visiting the vet — is reflected here instantly.

What you can do here

  • Monitor all five of your pet's vital stats at a glance
  • Track every dollar you spend on your pet, broken down by category
  • See which tasks you've completed today and what still needs doing
  • Get AI-generated care tips and spending analysis personalized to your pet
  • Chat with the AI assistant any time for quick advice

How to get the most out of it

1
Sign in with Google

Your pet data is tied to your account. Use the same Google account as your companion app.

2
Select your pet

Use the dropdown in the sidebar to switch between pets if you have more than one.

3
Check in daily

Stats decay over time. Reviewing your dashboard each day helps you catch low health or happiness before it becomes a problem.

4
Use the AI tab weekly

The AI analysis gives a fresh summary of your spending trends and pet condition — great for a quick weekly review.

All data shown here is live from Firebase — there's no manual entry needed. Just use your pet app normally and the dashboard updates automatically.

Dashboard Overview

A tour of every section of the dashboard and what it shows you.

The four tabs

  • Overview — Your home base. Top-level stats, quick AI insights, spending breakdown, and recent transactions all in one place.
  • Financial — Detailed budget tracking: donut chart, cumulative spending line graph, and how far you are through your monthly budget.
  • Health & Mood — All five stat gauges, a side-by-side bar chart, and milestone cards for level, XP, and days since adoption.
  • AI Insights — Full AI-written summary, bullet-point recommendations, alerts, and a spending forecast for the rest of the month.

The four stat cards (top of Overview)

Total Spent

All spending recorded this calendar month.

Budget Remaining

How much of your monthly budget is left.

Pet Happiness

Current happiness % from the app.

Coins

In-game coins earned through care tasks.

Switching pets

If you have multiple pets registered in the app, use the Active Pet dropdown at the top of the sidebar to switch between them. All charts and stats update instantly.

The dashboard is read-only. All data is managed through the companion pet app — you can't edit stats or transactions directly here.

Understanding Your Pet's Stats

Each stat reflects a different aspect of your pet's wellbeing. Keep them all high!

💚
Health (0–100)

Overall physical condition. Drops if your pet goes unfed, ungroomed, or skips vet visits. Below 30 is critical.

😊
Happiness (0–100)

Emotional state. Boosted by play, toys, and quality time. Falls when your pet is lonely or bored.

🍽️
Fullness (0–100)

Hunger level. Drops steadily over time. Feed your pet in the app to keep this above 50.

⚡
Energy (0–100)

Vitality and alertness. Recovers with rest and sleep tasks. Low energy means your pet can't enjoy activities.

❤️
Bond (0–100)

Your relationship strength with your pet. Grows slowly through consistent daily care. The hardest stat to build but the most rewarding.

Stat rating scale

  • 90–100 — Excellent: Your pet is thriving. Keep it up!
  • 70–89 — Good: Everything is comfortable. Minor improvements possible.
  • 50–69 — Fair: Getting into the yellow zone. Take action soon.
  • 30–49 — Low: Your pet needs attention. Open the app and complete tasks.
  • 0–29 — Critical: Urgent! Extended neglect here can affect other stats too.
Stats decay at different rates. Fullness drops the fastest (check in at least twice a day), while Bond changes the most slowly over weeks of consistent care.

Daily Care Tasks

Complete tasks in the app every day to keep your pet happy and healthy.

What are tasks?

Tasks are daily activities you perform in the companion app — things like feeding, playing, grooming, and sleeping. Each completed task raises one or more stats and awards XP. The dashboard shows you which tasks were completed today in the Tasks Completed card on the Overview tab.

Common tasks and their effects

  • Feed — Raises Fullness significantly. Also gives a small Health boost.
  • Play — Raises Happiness and Bond. Costs a little Energy.
  • Groom — Raises Health and Happiness. Counts as a care interaction for Bond.
  • Sleep / Rest — Fully restores Energy. Should be done at least once daily.
  • Vet Visit — Big Health boost. Costs money but prevents serious health drops.
  • Give Treat — Quick Happiness spike. Best used when happiness is in the Fair range.
  • Train — Awards bonus XP. Requires decent Energy to perform.

Why completing tasks every day matters

Stats decay passively over time whether you interact or not. Skipping a full day will noticeably drop Fullness and Happiness. Skipping multiple days can push Health into the critical range. The dashboard's Tasks Completed card is a quick sanity check — if it shows 0, open the app.

A good daily routine: Feed → Play → Groom → Sleep. This covers all five stats and takes just a few minutes in the app.

Leveling Up & XP

Your pet earns experience points for every care interaction. Watch them grow!

How XP works

Every completed task in the app awards a certain amount of XP (experience points). When your pet's XP reaches the threshold for the next level, they level up automatically. The Health & Mood tab shows your current level, XP progress, and how much XP is needed to level up.

XP rewards by task type

  • Feed — Small XP reward (routine care)
  • Play — Medium XP reward (active engagement)
  • Groom — Small XP reward
  • Sleep — Minimal XP (recovery task)
  • Vet Visit — Large XP reward (reflects real cost and effort)
  • Train — Largest XP reward (dedicated skill-building)

What leveling up does

Higher-level pets unlock new tasks, accessories, and interactions in the companion app. Leveling up also awards Coins, which can be spent on in-app items. The dashboard tracks your level but all rewards are delivered in the app.

The fastest way to level up is to combine daily tasks with regular Train sessions. Train gives the most XP per interaction but requires your pet to have decent Energy first.

Budget & Spending Tracking

Keep your pet care costs under control with real-time budget monitoring.

How spending is tracked

Every time you perform an action in the app that has a real-world cost equivalent — buying food, toys, grooming supplies, or scheduling vet visits — a transaction is logged to your account. The dashboard reads these transactions and categorises them automatically.

The four spending categories

  • Food & Supplies — Kibble, treats, bowls, leashes, and everyday consumables.
  • Vet & Health — Check-ups, vaccinations, medications, and emergency visits.
  • Toys & Activities — Toys, training classes, grooming, and entertainment.
  • Other — Anything that doesn't fit the above categories.

Reading the Financial tab

  • The donut chart shows your spending split across categories, plus how much budget is remaining.
  • The transaction timeline shows your cumulative spending as a running total — the red dashed line is your monthly budget ceiling.
  • The budget bar at the bottom gives an instant percentage view. It turns orange/red when you're over 90%.
Transactions reset at the start of each calendar month. Historical data from prior months is not shown in the current view — only the current month's spending is displayed.

AI Assistant & Insights

Let the AI help you understand your pet's condition and make smarter care decisions.

AI Insights tab

The AI Insights tab generates a fresh analysis every time you load it. It reads your pet's live stats, your spending data, and your transaction history, then produces three sections:

  • Summary — A plain-English overview of your pet's current condition and your financial position.
  • Recommendations — Specific, actionable suggestions tailored to what your pet actually needs right now.
  • Alerts & Anomalies — Flags unusual patterns, like a stat that's been dropping fast or spending in a category that's unusually high.

The AI Chat assistant

Click the teal robot button in the bottom-right corner to open the chat panel. You can ask it anything about your pet — it has full context of your pet's current stats and your spending data, so answers are specific to your situation, not generic.

Good questions to try:

  • "My pet's energy is low — what should I do?"
  • "Am I spending too much on toys?"
  • "What tasks should I prioritize today?"
  • "How long until my pet levels up?"

Quick insights on the Overview

The three bullet points on the teal banner on the Overview tab are AI-generated at load time. They give you a fast snapshot of the most important things to know right now — budget status, happiness level, and the most pressing health concern — without needing to navigate to the full AI tab.

The AI generates a new analysis each time you visit the AI Insights tab. If stats change significantly after completing tasks in the app, reload the tab to get an updated read.
