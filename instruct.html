Your Virtual Pet Care Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Helix, your virtual pet companion platform. This guide will help you understand how to care for your pet and make the most of your experience.

Creating Your Account

  • Sign up using your email and password or Google account
  • Choose your first pet from Cat, Dog, or Bird
  • Give your pet a unique name
  • Start your journey with 500 coins and a monthly budget of 200 dollars

Adding More Pets

  • Click the floating plus button in the bottom right corner
  • Select a new pet type and give it a name
  • Switch between your pets using the pet bubbles in the control panel

Understanding Your Pet Stats

Your pet has four main stats that you need to monitor and maintain. Each stat decreases over time and requires different care actions.

Health

Overall wellbeing. Decreases when other stats are critically low. Restore with vet visits.

Happiness

Emotional state. Decreases over 24 hours without play. Restore by playing with toys.

Fullness

Hunger level. Decreases every 8 hours. Restore by feeding your pet.

Energy

Rest level. Decreases every 16 hours. Restore by letting your pet rest.

Critical Warning: If fullness drops below 10, health will decrease rapidly. Keep your pet well-fed to maintain good health.

Care Actions

You have four care actions available on the dashboard. Each action has different costs and effects.

Feed - 10 Dollars

  • Increases fullness by 30 points
  • Increases health by 5 points
  • Increases happiness by 10 points
  • Grants 10 experience points

Play with Toy - 15 Dollars

  • Increases happiness by 20 points
  • Decreases energy by 15 points
  • Decreases fullness by 10 points
  • Grants 15 experience points
  • Cannot be used if energy is below 20

Rest - Free

  • Increases energy by 40 points
  • Increases health by 5 points
  • Grants 5 experience points
  • Cannot be used if energy is above 80

Health Check at Vet - 50 Dollars

  • Restores health to 100 points
  • Grants 20 experience points
  • Provides diagnosis of other stat issues

Daily Tasks and Bonding

Complete daily tasks to earn coins and increase your bond level with your pet. Higher bond levels unlock new age stages.

Task System

  • Each pet type has unique tasks based on their personality
  • Tasks reward coins and bond points when completed
  • Tasks reset every 24 hours
  • Use the AI Tasks button to generate personalized tasks

Bond Levels and Aging

  • Baby: 0 to 24 bond points
  • Young: 25 to 49 bond points
  • Adult: 50 to 74 bond points
  • Senior: 75 to 100 bond points

Bond increases only through completing daily tasks together. There is no direct petting action. Focus on tasks to strengthen your relationship.

Experience and Leveling

Your pet gains experience points through care actions and tasks. Leveling up provides rewards and increases your pet's abilities.

Gaining Experience

  • Feed: 10 XP
  • Play: 15 XP
  • Rest: 5 XP
  • Vet Visit: 20 XP
  • Daily Tasks: Variable XP based on task difficulty

Level Up Rewards

  • 50 bonus coins
  • 20 point health increase
  • 20 point happiness increase
  • Experience requirement increases by 50 percent each level

Budget and Expense Tracking

Manage your monthly budget of 200 dollars. Track spending across categories to stay within your limits.

Expense Categories

  • Food and Supplies: Feeding your pet
  • Health Care: Veterinary visits
  • Toys and Activities: Play and entertainment

Budget Management

  • Monitor remaining budget in the expense tracker
  • Budget prevents spending when exceeded
  • All transactions are saved to your account history
  • Budget resets monthly

Warning: Once your monthly budget is exceeded, you cannot make any purchases until the next month. Plan your spending carefully.

Pet Traits and Personalities

Each pet type has unique traits and specialized tasks that match their personality.

Cat

  • Traits: Independent, Playful, Curious
  • Tasks: Play with toys, Pet and cuddle, Explore the house
  • Special Item: Cat Treats

Dog

  • Traits: Loyal, Energetic, Social
  • Tasks: Go for a run, Play fetch, Practice commands
  • Special Item: Dog Biscuits

Bird

  • Traits: Social, Intelligent, Vocal
  • Tasks: Talk and interact, Teach new words, Play with toys
  • Special Item: Spray Millet

AI Features

Helix includes artificial intelligence features to enhance your pet care experience.

AI Generated Tasks

  • Click the AI Tasks button to generate personalized daily tasks
  • Tasks adapt to your pet's current stats and needs
  • Each task is unique and reflects your pet's personality
  • AI tasks reward the same as standard tasks

Chat with Your Pet

  • Use premade chat options to interact with your pet
  • Your pet responds based on their current stats and mood
  • Pets express happiness when stats are high
  • Pets express concerns when stats are low

Random Events

  • Events occur periodically based on your pet's condition
  • Events can affect stats positively or negatively
  • Special memory events trigger after 30 days
  • Events appear in the events panel and disappear after 15 seconds

Tips for Success

  • Check your pet regularly to maintain healthy stats
  • Complete daily tasks to earn coins and increase bond
  • Monitor your budget to avoid running out of funds
  • Keep fullness above 30 to prevent health decline
  • Use rest action when energy is low to maintain health
  • Level up your pet by completing care actions consistently
  • Switch between multiple pets to care for them all
  • Use AI tasks for variety and personalization
  • Read random events to learn more about your pet's daily life
  • Plan your spending to make your budget last the full month

Account and Data

Your account data is securely stored in the cloud and accessible from any device.

What Gets Saved

  • All pet stats and progress
  • Coins and budget information
  • Task completion status
  • Transaction history
  • Multiple pets and their individual data
  • Bond levels and age progression

Account Security

  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Use a strong password for your account
  • Your data is private and not shared with other users
